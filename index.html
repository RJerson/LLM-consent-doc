What is the purpose of the project?

A Large Language Model (LLM) is a type of machine learning model that uses a neural network with billions of parameters to process and understand natural language by capturing the syntax and semantics of human language, as well as general knowledge and facts about the world. This allows it to perform tasks such as text generation, classification, and question answering. In the field of project management, characterized for having a combination of general knowledge and a dynamic nature, these tools have been demonstrated to be helpful for planning, communication, and learning, among many other applications. One example of this is the widely popular application ChatGPT based on the large language model GPT-3.5. However, to this date, there is a lack of empirical research on the applications and impacts of LLMs for project management, which impedes their adoption and hinders the development of more efficient project management practices, especially from the latest and evolved models such as GPT-4, that have demonstrated a remarkable improvement in the technology’s capabilities and alignment. Thus, this project’s purpose is to narrow the knowledge gap on how LLMs can be leveraged to enhance project management by analysing the capabilities and identifying the potential challenges and limitations for successful integration.

Finally, this research plans to be a dissertation for a master's degree in Engineering project management with the duration of 4 months.

Why have I been chosen?

You and other participants were chosen based on their expertise and experience in project management. to gather opinions and experiences from a diverse group of individuals with varying levels of experience to assess the performance of Large Language Models in project management. This study plans to survey a minimum of 50 people from different levels of experience in project management about their opinions and experiences. The study will be conducted in developed countries where project management is widely recognized and practiced.

What do I have to do? / What will happen to me if I take part?

You will be asked to complete a questionnaire once, with a duration of approximately 15 to 20 minutes. This questionnaire will cover first closed ended demographic and career related questions, secondly a survey about the participant’s usage of LLMs, thirdly an evaluation of accuracy and relevance for LLMs generated text of project management case studies’ solutions, and finally an open-ended questionnaire for completing any opinions and feedback. Although the first, second and third section of the questionnaire is close ended. You will have the last part to discuss in-depth opinions and feedback about the technology, its solutions and the questionnaire itself. We expect comprehensive and reasonable answers, related to the technology and the field of project management with justifications of any expected opinion. The data will be analysed in a mixed approach both using quantitative and qualitative methods. This is an online form and only the answers and text will be recorder. There is no financial incentive to complete the form, as this is dissertation research in order to achieve a master’s degree As this is an online questionnaire about the usage of a particular technology in your field done anonymously, there is no expected health or lifestyle related consequences or restrictions in any way, shape or form. It is your decision to use this technology in your work or personal activities, and any unforeseeable consequences will not be the responsibility of this investigation.

What are the possible disadvantages and risks of taking part?

This technology generates text almost indistinguishable from those generated by humans, and some users have experienced discomfort, especially if they have never tried this before. Fortunately, the past year has seen a wide adoption and popularity of chatbots using large language models to process text. Therefore, you should be already familiar or adapted with this technology. Any risks of disclosure of personal information, data breaches and privacy are minimized by complying with the confidentiality and anonymity of the research and taking multiple measure to guarantee the security of the data, as explained below. Finally, it is expected that no participant will expose any personal information in the open-ended questions and that you will take the questionnaire in a secure access to the web.

What are the possible benefits of taking part?

This paper aims to understand the capabilities and impacts of LLMs in project management. Therefore, by taking this survey, you will have the added value to understand better how this technology can be implemented in your daily work, and what are the main limitations in a structured way. Overall, it is hoped that this work will make a systematic understanding on the field’s part in this AI technology and how can be leveraged or limited to further improve the field.
